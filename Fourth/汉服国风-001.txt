Snowflakes swirled underground and fell on the hair and clothes of the beautiful woman in Hanfu. She stood in the snow as if she were a beautiful picture. She gently stroked the snowflakes, her mouth evoked a smile, and said softly, "This snowy world is really a pure world. The combination of Hanfu and snow scenes makes it even more ancient. In this snowy scene, I feel the peace and relaxation of my mind, as if I were in a dust-free world. I hope time can stay in this beautiful moment forever."
